H1 Classification Patterns

Six distinct patterns for generating the perfect opening sentence based on article intent.

Definitional
"[Entity] is [definition]."

Example: What is X?
How-To
"To [verb], [action/outcome]."

Example: How to...
List
"There are [N] key [items]..."

Example: Top X, Benefits of...
Boolean
"Yes/No, [claim], if [condition]..."

Example: Can/Is/Does...
Comparative
"The [best] for [user] is..."

Example: Best/Most...
Other/Brand
"[Entity] helps [user] [outcome]..."

Example: Brand pages

Prompt Caching

Async Concurrency

Processes 5 keywords in parallel with intelligent rate limiting (60 req/min, 60k tokens/min).

Resume Support

Checkpoint state saved after each batch—interrupted runs resume exactly where they left off.
