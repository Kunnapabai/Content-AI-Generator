Research Phase Structure

Sequential phases building from fundamentals to buyer enablement.

1

Fundamentals

Core concepts, definitions, and background knowledge required for understanding the topic.

2

Materials & Types

Product categories, material options, and classification systems.

3

Specifications

Technical specs, standards (JIS/GB/ASTM), and performance metrics.

4

Selection Criteria

Decision factors, comparison guides, and evaluation frameworks.

5

Installation

Implementation guides, best practices, and maintenance procedures.

6

Buyer Enablement

Pricing, warranties, supplier info, and purchase guidance.

Operational Advantages

Checkpoint Recovery

Automatic progress tracking with JSON checkpoints allows interrupted sessions to resume exactly where they left off.

Multilingual Queries

Generates Boolean search queries in English, Japanese (JIS), and Chinese (GB) for comprehensive source coverage.

Evidence Classification

Tags all recommendations with evidence levels (Strong/Moderate/Preliminary) for source quality transparency.

Concurrent Processing

Processes up to 8 keywords simultaneously with intelligent rate limiting to maximize throughput.
