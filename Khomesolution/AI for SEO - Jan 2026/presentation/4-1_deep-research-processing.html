Key Features

Resume Capability

Persistent state tracking allows interrupted sessions to resume exactly where they left off, preserving conversation URLs.

Concurrent Processing

Process up to 5 keywords simultaneously with intelligent job scheduling and staggered launches to avoid rate limits.

Robust Error Handling

Automatic retry logic with exponential backoff, stage-based recovery, and detailed error logging for debugging.

Profile Isolation

Dedicated Chrome automation profile keeps research sessions separate from personal browsing data.

Follow-up Prompts

Automated follow-up questions from specs/follow-up-deepresearch.md to enhance research depth and coverage.

Auto-Cleanup

Completed jobs older than 24 hours are automatically removed from state to keep the system clean.
