Pipeline Advantages

Parallel Processing

Process multiple keywords simultaneously with --parallel N flag. Each keyword runs through all 4 stages independently with thread-safe progress tracking.

Smart Question Selection

Intelligent heading extraction with rules: always include leaf nodes, H3/H4, and question-like H2s (ends with ?, contains WH-words, Thai interrogatives).

Conditional Citations

Citation injection only when both conditions met: "Included citation" in arguments AND citable sources exist in context. Clean separation of concerns.

Resume Capability

Skips already-generated answers unless --overwrite is set. Real-time progress shows completed vs pending questions per keyword.
