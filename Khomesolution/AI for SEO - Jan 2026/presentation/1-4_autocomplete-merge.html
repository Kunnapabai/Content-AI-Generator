Merge Rules
  • Exact keyword matches are deduplicated (case-insensitive)
  • When duplicates found, keep record with highest search volume
  • If volumes equal, prefer Ahrefs data (more complete metrics)
  • Unicode normalization (NFC) applied before comparison
  • Whitespace trimmed and standardized
