Optimization Techniques

Multi-pass compression engine applying intelligent rules to maximize token reduction.

Whitespace Normalization

Collapse excessive blank lines, trim trailing spaces, and standardize indentation to single spaces.

~8% reduction

Filler Word Removal

Strip "basically", "essentially", "in order to", "it is important to note" and similar padding.

~12% reduction

Deduplication

Detect and merge duplicate paragraphs, repeated citations, and redundant bullet points.

~15% reduction

Header Consolidation

Flatten excessive nesting, remove empty sections, and merge adjacent similar headers.

~5% reduction

URL Shortening

Convert verbose URLs to reference-style links with numbered citations at document end.

~10% reduction

Citation Normalization

Standardize citation format, remove duplicate sources, and consolidate inline references.

~5% reduction
